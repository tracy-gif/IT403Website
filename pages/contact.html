Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What do you use to make your art?
A: I mostly use Paint Tool Sai and Photoshop for making art and a wacom intuos pro small tablet for actually drawing digitally.

Q: Can I give you feedback during the commissioning process?
A: Yes! I am here to draw something that you will love so I encourage any feedback.

Q: How will I be able to see the process?
A: If you are the commissioner, all correspondence and WIP photo will be made via emails. Otherwise, the art may be posted in my gallery and can be seen there.

Q: Can you do physical prints and send them through mail?
A: Unfortunately, as of right now, I do not make physical prints and the photos will be sent through mail. However, if you'd like to make a print of my art, I am fine with that, as long as you do not claim the art as your own or try to sell it for profit.

Q: What if I want to request something you don't have listed as available?
A: Feel free to contact me with any concerns you may have! If I don't have an answer listed on the website, the best bet is to contactly me directly and I'll be happy to answer any burning questions you may have. ☺
