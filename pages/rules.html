Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you agree to the terms below. Therefore, to ensure a smooth commission process for both the artist and client please read these rules carefully:

PROCESS

  • The commission form must be completed in order to be considered. Any requests made not using the form will be ignored.
  • Once the form is complete, an email will be sent to you regarding acceptance of the of the form.
  • To ensure fairness, the commission will be paid in two separate payments: 50% up front and the remaining 50% once completed.
  • Work in Progress ( WIPS ) will be provided at different sections of progress. These are typically:
    • Intial Sketch
    • Line Art
    • Base Colors
    • Shading
    • Effects
    If there are any issues with what I have drawn for you, I am more than willing to redraw and edit it until it is satisfactory, as long as the work is less than 65% complete.

COPYRIGHT/USAGE

  • Commissioned works will have a watermark/signature. Do not remove or tamper with the signature, unless otherwise discussed.
  • Do not copy, trace or claim the piece as your own work.
  • You may use the work as graphics for websites or public projects however, please provide credit where credit is due.

CANCELLATIONS/REFUNDS

  • Once 50% of the work has been done. I will not refund the first payment. Otherwise, if the you wish to cancel before 50% of the work has been done or I cannot fufill your request for any reason, I will happily refund you.

DISCLAIMER

  • I am not obligated to accept any requests made.
  • Any of these rules are subject to change at my discretion.

I understand these conditions and wish to commission you.
